Samantha Steve, Hakim James, John Bolu, Inneh Happiness, Isa Gideon better known by their stage name "We Five", is a Africa Orchestra which has been active since 2010 till date.

We Five is formed when like-minded musicians kept running into each other in university at their home country (Nigeria) and decided to form a band. Their name "We Five" is formed because it is a group of Five(5).

We Five is a musical group that gives rhythm and harmony which find their way into the inward places of the soul.

In We Five orchestra, we produce classical musics and each living instrument is essential to the complementary and harmonious playing of the whole

We Five is a corporate friendly show for any audience, but still designed to help people take themselves a bit less serious. We mixes music with comedy to relieve audiences with a stressful day.

Music is an essential part of almost every event. We can help celebrate the moment. We provide the perfect backdrop for a city anniversary celebration, a night under the stars, Orchestral holiday music and more.
